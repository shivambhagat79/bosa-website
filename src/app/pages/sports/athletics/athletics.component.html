55th Inter IIT sports meet

IIT Ropar Athletics Team secured 10th place among 23 IITs in the Athletics Championship. The highlight of overall performance of the team is as follows:

  • Amit Kumar Chhipa won Bronze Medal in the Discus Throw (Men) final and secured 4 th place in the Shot Put (Men) final.
  • Sawan Kumar Meena secured 6 th place in the 800 m (Men) final and 8 th place in the 5000 m (Men) final.
  • Kushal Agarwal secured 8 th place in the Discus Throw (Women) final. • IIT Ropar team secured 8 th place in the 4×400 m (Men) final.
  • Yogita secured 10th place in the 1500 m (Women) final.
  • Sayed Musaraf secured 10th place in the Long Jump (Men) final.
