    Words from the General Secretary

    As General Secretary of Boards of Sports Activities, my aim is to foster a vibrant sports culture, provide inclusive opportunities, and support our athletes in achieving excellence. Together, let's embrace the spirit of sportsmanship and inspire greatness in every individual.

    Our Mission

    "Sports do not build character. They reveal it." It is often said that the key to a sharp mind is a healthy body and therefore to make its students "healthy, wealthy and wise" IIT Ropar lays a huge emphasis on promoting sports activities among it's students. And thus the sports become an integral part of the education process of the students. The students have various sports facilities which they enjoy every day in campus. Presently, IIT Ropar has one cricket field, three cricket practise pitches, three clay tennis courts, two volleyball courts, a football field, a hockey field, a gymnasium, two table tennis tables, a basketball court and a ground for different athletic events.

    Aarohan

    Aarohan, the annual sports fest of IIT Ropar, is an exhilarating celebration of athleticism, team spirit, and competition. Held with great enthusiasm and fervor, Aarohan brings together students from across the country to showcase their sporting prowess in a wide range of events. From track and field to indoor and outdoor sports, the festival offers a diverse array of competitions that cater to every sporting interest. Participants battle it out in an electrifying atmosphere, supported by cheering crowds and the camaraderie that comes with being part of a prestigious institute like IIT Ropar. Aarohan serves as a platform for athletes to test their skills, forge new friendships, and create lasting memories. With its vibrant energy and unwavering passion for sports, Aarohan has become an unmissable event on the calendar of sports enthusiasts in the region. We had a total of over 600 participants, with over 80 girls and 500 boys, who competed fiercely in 12 sporting events. It was a pleasure to see the participants from 11 different colleges, coming from as far as 1500 km to participate in the fest.     Inter IIT

    The Inter IIT Sports Meet, a grand annual extravaganza, unites the finest athletes from esteemed Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) in a display of unparalleled sporting prowess. With a resplendent blend of healthy competition, unwavering sportsmanship, and an extraordinary showcase of talent across diverse disciplines, it is a testament to the indomitable spirit that drives the participants to greatness. In the 55th edition, held with great fervor in Roorkee and Delhi, IIT Ropar proudly emerged as a beacon of excellence. Securing a remarkable 7th position in the overall championship, their achievements shone brightly with an impressive conquest in the esteemed female tennis championship. Additionally, the illustrious IIT Ropar athletes soared to new heights, clinching the bronze medal in the illustrious discuss throw and table tennis events, while demonstrating their mettle with a remarkable 4th position in cricket and shotput. These extraordinary achievements exemplify the unwavering dedication, perseverance, and relentless pursuit of greatness displayed by the phenomenal athletes from IIT Ropar, making them an epitome of sporting prowess and a source of immense pride.
