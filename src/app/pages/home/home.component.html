    Our Mission

    "Sports do not build character. They reveal it." It is often said that the key to a sharp mind is a healthy body and therefore to make its students "healthy, wealthy and wise" IIT Ropar lays a huge emphasis on promoting sports activities among it's students. And thus the sports become an integral part of the education process of the students. The students have various sports facilities which they enjoy every day in campus. Presently, IIT Ropar has one cricket field, three cricket practise pitches, three clay tennis courts, two volleyball courts, a football field, a hockey field, a gymnasium, two table tennis tables, a basketball court, a badminton court and a ground for different athletic events.
